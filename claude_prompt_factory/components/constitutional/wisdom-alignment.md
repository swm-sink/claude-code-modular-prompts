# Contemplative Wisdom for Superalignment

Advanced AI alignment framework integrating contemplative wisdom traditions with cutting-edge AI safety techniques for intrinsic morality and resilient ethical behavior.

## Command

`/wisdom-superalignment`

## Purpose

Implement a contemplative wisdom approach to AI alignment that builds intrinsic morality into AI cognitive architecture through four foundational principles: mindfulness, emptiness, non-duality, and boundless care for robust ethical behavior.
